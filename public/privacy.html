Privacy Policy

iDew Condensation Monitor — Storm Innovations
Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Storm Innovations ("we," "our," or "us") operates the iDew Condensation Monitor mobile application ("the App") and associated hardware devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App and iDew devices.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely via our authentication provider).

Device & Sensor Data: iDew hardware devices collect environmental sensor readings including temperature, humidity, dew point, and surface temperature. This data is transmitted to our cloud servers and associated with your account.

Environment Metadata: During device setup, you may optionally provide information about your installation environment, including location type (e.g., basement, garage), pipe material, room type, ZIP code, and whether HVAC equipment is nearby.

Location Data: We collect a ZIP code (if provided) solely to retrieve local weather data for the device's location. We do not collect precise GPS location.

Device Information: We collect device identifiers (MAC address), firmware version, and connectivity status (online/offline) for your iDew hardware devices.

Usage Data: We may collect basic app usage analytics such as screen views and feature usage to improve the App experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the iDew monitoring service
  • Display real-time and historical sensor readings on your dashboard
  • Send you alerts when condensation risk is detected or your device status changes
  • Retrieve local weather data based on the device ZIP code to enhance condensation risk assessment
  • Improve and develop new features for the App and hardware
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard cloud infrastructure providers. Sensor data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Account credentials are managed through a secure third-party authentication service with encrypted password storage.

We retain sensor reading data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, authentication, and database providers that help us operate the App (e.g., Supabase, InfluxDB). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Weather Services: The device ZIP code is sent to third-party weather APIs (Open-Meteo) to retrieve local weather conditions. No personal information beyond the ZIP code is shared.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

6. Bluetooth & Permissions

The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) during initial device setup to securely transfer WiFi credentials to your iDew device. BLE is only active during the provisioning process and is not used for ongoing data collection. The App requests Bluetooth permissions solely for this purpose.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account through the App
  • Correction: Update your account information and device settings at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual devices and their associated data from the App, or delete your entire account and all associated data from within the App's Account settings
  • Data Export: Export your sensor data as CSV from the App's Device settings

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
